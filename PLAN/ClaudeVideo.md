# PetDay Demo Video — Production Script v2

> Target: **2 min 00 s** | Tone: Cinematic + Heartwarming + Tech-credible
> Audience: Gemini Hackathon judges (technical + product sense)

---

## Overview & Structure

| Act | Theme | Duration | Type |
|-----|-------|----------|------|
| 1 | Emotional Hook — The Wonder | 0:00 – 0:18 | AI-generated video |
| 2 | The Camera — Pet's Eyes | 0:18 – 0:30 | AI-generated video |
| 3 | The Pain Point + PetDay Reveal | 0:30 – 0:45 | AI-gen + Motion graphic |
| 4 | Product Demo (Core) | 0:45 – 1:40 | Screen recording |
| 5 | Emotional Payoff | 1:40 – 2:00 | AI-generated video |

## ACT 1 — The Wonder (0:00 – 0:18)

### Visual

- **Camera**: Over-the-shoulder (OTS) of a pet owner standing in a field during golden hour.
- **Subject**: A golden retriever running away from camera across a sunlit lawn. Backlit — fur edges glowing, each strand catching light. Slow motion (48fps feel).
- **Lens**: Shallow depth of field. Owner's shoulder/silhouette soft in foreground, dog sharp in mid-ground, sun flaring in background.
- **Color**: Warm amber tones. Cinematic color grade.

### Voiceover (EN)

> "Every pet owner has had this moment — watching them run off, and wondering...
> What do they see? What are they thinking? What does their day really look like?"

### Voiceover (ZH — for reference)

> "每个宠物主人都有过这样的时刻——看着它奔跑的背影，好奇它眼中的世界是什么样的？它在想什么？它的一天，究竟是怎么度过的？"

### Production Notes

- **AI Video Tool**: Kling AI or Runway Gen-3 (prompt: "Over-the-shoulder shot of person watching golden retriever running across sunset lawn, backlit glowing fur, cinematic shallow DOF, golden hour, slow motion")
- **Music**: Soft piano + ambient pads, building subtly. Reference: Thomas Newman style.
- **Pacing**: Slow, contemplative. Let the image breathe. One continuous shot if possible.

---

## ACT 2 — The Camera (0:18 – 0:30)

### Visual

**Shot A (0:18 – 0:23)**: Close-up of owner's hands gently clipping a small camera onto the golden retriever's collar. The dog looks up, tail wagging. Warm indoor/porch light.

**Shot B (0:23 – 0:30)**: Hard cut to **Pet POV** (first-person). Sudden energy shift — shaky, low-angle, wide-lens footage. The dog bursts through a door into a backyard, sniffing grass, running along a fence. Raw, authentic feel. Speed ramps from normal to slightly fast.

### Voiceover (EN)

> "So we gave them a camera."

*(Beat — let the POV footage speak for itself for 3–4 seconds with natural sounds: panting, grass rustling, tags jingling)*

### Voiceover (ZH)

> "于是，我们给它戴上了一台相机。"

### Production Notes

- **Shot A**: AI-generated (Kling/Runway: "Close-up hands attaching small camera to golden retriever collar, warm light, shallow DOF")
- **Shot B**: Use real POV footage if available (raw GoPro pet cam), or AI-generate a shaky low-angle dog running POV. Raw quality is intentional here — don't stabilize.
- **Sound design**: Diegetic sounds (dog panting, collar jingling) mixed louder than music. Creates immersion.
- **Key transition**: The cut from owner's hands (Act 2A) to POV (Act 2B) is the "aha" moment — the audience now understands what a pet camera is.

---

## ACT 3 — The Pain Point + PetDay Reveal (0:30 – 0:45)

### Visual

**Phase 1 — The Problem (0:30 – 0:38)**:

The chaotic POV footage from Act 2 continues for 1 second, then **zooms out** — the footage shrinks and lands inside an iPad screen. Camera pulls back to reveal a person sitting on a couch, holding the iPad, looking overwhelmed. They swipe through the timeline — it's all the same shaky nothing. They set the iPad down with a sigh.

- **Text overlay** (optional): A subtle file size indicator — `"2h 47min | 12.3 GB"` — appears on the iPad screen before it's set down.

**Phase 2 — The Reveal (0:38 – 0:45)**:

- **Hard cut to black**. 1 second of silence.
- PetDay logo fades in (center screen, minimal animation — just opacity 0→1).
- Below the logo, a single line types on:

> **"PetDay turns raw pet videos into a daily story."**

- Logo holds for 2 seconds, then transitions to the product demo.

### Voiceover (EN)

> *(Phase 1)* "But reality? Hours of shaky footage. No one has time for this."

*(Phase 2 — No voiceover. Let the tagline do the work. Music shifts: piano resolves into a confident, forward-moving beat.)*

### Voiceover (ZH)

> "但现实是——几个小时晃动的画面，谁有时间去翻看？"

### Production Notes

- **The iPad transition**: This is the most technically demanding AI-gen shot. Alternative approach: screen-record the POV footage playing on an actual iPad, film yourself setting it down. More authentic and easier to execute.
- **Logo animation**: Keep it dead simple. Apple-style. No spinning 3D logos. Just: fade in, hold, fade out.
- **Music transition**: This is where the score shifts from "emotional/wondering" to "confident/tech". A single low bass note or synth pad works well as a bridge.

- **Mouse**: Move deliberately and slowly. Pause 0.5s on each element before moving.

## ACT 5 — Emotional Payoff (1:40 – 2:00)

### Visual

**Montage (1:40 – 1:53)**: 2–3 short AI-generated clips (3–4 seconds each), styled as "highlights from a pet's day":

1. **Clip 1**: A golden retriever sniffing a butterfly on a flower, then looking up at the camera with a head tilt. *(Wonder)*
2. **Clip 2**: Two dogs meeting nose-to-nose in a park, tails wagging. *(Friendship)*
3. **Clip 3**: A dog sprinting full speed across a field, directly toward the camera (toward the owner), leaping with joy. *(Love)* — This is the final emotional hit.

Each clip has a **subtle pet "inner voice" caption** at the bottom (styled like the PetDay AI diary):

- Clip 1: *"What IS that thing? It flies! I must investigate."*
- Clip 2: *"Frosty! My favorite. Same time tomorrow?"*
- Clip 3: *"There you are. My favorite human. INCOMING!"*

**Closing (1:53 – 2:00)**:

- Cut to black after the dog leaps.
- Beat of silence (0.5s).
- PetDay logo appears.
- Tagline fades in below:

> **"See life from their side."**

- URL below: `petday.cc`
- Hold for 3 seconds. Music resolves.

### Voiceover (EN)

> *(Over the montage, warm and genuine)*:
> "Every walk. Every friend. Every quiet moment of wonder. PetDay turns the ordinary into a story worth keeping."

*(Silence over the final logo. Let it land.)*

### Voiceover (ZH)

> "每一次奔跑，每一个朋友，每一个安静的好奇瞬间。PetDay，让平凡的每一帧，都变成值得珍藏的故事。"

### Production Notes

- **Pet voice captions**: Use a handwritten-style font, slightly transparent white, bottom-center. Appears 0.5s after each clip starts, fades out 0.3s before clip ends.
- **AI Video Tools**: Kling AI excels at animal motion. Use specific prompts for each clip.
- **The final leap**: This is the money shot. Generate multiple versions and pick the one with the most joyful energy.
- **Tagline decision**: Use **"See life from their side."** as the primary English tagline. It's short, memorable, and captures the POV concept. The Chinese version "让平凡的每一帧，都变成值得珍藏的故事" works as the VO closing line (more poetic, fits the spoken medium).

- **Pacing**: Leave breathing room. Don't rush through the demo section — let the UI speak.

## 第 1 幕 — 好奇与向往 (0:00 – 0:18)

### 画面

- **机位**: 主人越肩视角（OTS），站在黄金时段的草地上。
- **主体**: 金毛在逆光下奔跑远去，毛发边缘丝丝缕缕地发光。慢动作质感（48fps）。
- **镜头**: 浅景深。前景是主人的肩膀/剪影（虚焦），中景是奔跑的狗（实焦），背景是落日光晕。
- **色调**: 暖琥珀色调，电影级调色。

### 旁白

> "每个宠物主人都有过这样的时刻——看着它奔跑的背影，好奇它眼中的世界是什么样的？它在想什么？它的一天，究竟是怎么度过的？"

### 制作备注

- **AI 视频工具**: Kling AI 或 Runway Gen-3（提示词: "Over-the-shoulder shot of person watching golden retriever running across sunset lawn, backlit glowing fur, cinematic shallow DOF, golden hour, slow motion"）
- **音乐**: 柔和钢琴 + 环境弦乐铺垫，缓缓推进。参考风格: Thomas Newman。
- **节奏**: 慢、沉思。让画面自己说话。尽量用一个连续长镜头。

## 第 2 幕 — 戴上相机 (0:18 – 0:30)

### 画面

**镜头 A (0:18 – 0:23)**: 特写——主人的手轻轻将小摄像头卡扣到金毛的项圈上。狗抬头看主人，尾巴摇摆。温暖的室内/门廊光线。

**镜头 B (0:23 – 0:30)**: 硬切到**宠物第一人称视角 (POV)**。能量骤变——抖动的、低角度的、广角镜头画面。狗冲出门跑进后院，嗅草、沿着围栏奔跑。原始、真实的质感。速度从正常到略微加速。

### 旁白

> "于是，我们给它戴上了一台相机。"

*(停顿——让 POV 画面自己说话 3–4 秒，只保留自然声音: 喘息声、草丛沙沙声、项圈叮当声)*

### 制作备注

- **镜头 A**: AI 生成（Kling/Runway: "Close-up hands attaching small camera to golden retriever collar, warm light, shallow DOF"）
- **镜头 B**: 如有真实宠物 POV 素材（GoPro 拍的），优先使用；否则 AI 生成低角度狗跑视角。**故意保留抖动，不要做防抖处理**。
- **声音设计**: 现场同期声（狗喘气、项圈响）音量高于音乐，制造沉浸感。
- **关键转场**: 从主人的手（镜头 A）硬切到 POV 画面（镜头 B）是全片的"恍然大悟"时刻——观众瞬间理解了"宠物摄像头"是什么。

---

## 第 3 幕 — 痛点 + PetDay 亮相 (0:30 – 0:45)

### 画面

**阶段 1 — 痛点 (0:30 – 0:38)**:

第 2 幕的混乱 POV 画面继续播放 1 秒，然后**缩小**——画面缩进一个 iPad 屏幕里。镜头拉远，露出一个人坐在沙发上拿着 iPad，表情无奈。滑动时间线——全是一样的抖动画面。叹气，放下 iPad。

- **文字叠加**（可选）: iPad 屏幕上出现一个微妙的文件信息——`"2h 47min | 12.3 GB"`——然后 iPad 被放下。

**阶段 2 — 产品揭幕 (0:38 – 0:45)**:

- **硬切到黑屏**。1 秒静默。
- PetDay Logo 淡入（屏幕中央，极简动画——纯透明度 0→1）。
- Logo 下方，一行文字打字机效果出现:

> **"PetDay turns raw pet videos into a daily story."**

- Logo 停留 2 秒，转场到产品演示。

### 旁白

> *(阶段 1)* "但现实是——几个小时晃动的画面，谁有时间去翻看？"

*(阶段 2——不加旁白。让文字自己发力。音乐变化: 钢琴收束，转为自信、向前推进的节拍。)*

### 制作备注

- **iPad 转场**: 这是 AI 生成难度最高的镜头。替代方案: 在真实 iPad 上播放 POV 素材，用手机拍下自己放下 iPad 的动作。更真实也更容易执行。
- **Logo 动画**: 越简单越好。Apple 风格。不要旋转 3D Logo。就是: 淡入、停留、淡出。
- **音乐转场**: 这里是配乐从"情感/沉思"切换到"自信/科技"的节点。一个低沉的贝斯音或合成器铺垫可以很好地做桥接。

## Kling AI 使用技巧

| 设置项 | 推荐值 |
|--------|--------|
| Model | Kling 2.1 Master |
| Mode | Professional |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 |
| Duration | 5s (片段 2-7) / 10s (片段 1) |
| Camera Movement | 片段 1, 5, 6: Static / 片段 3: Handheld / 片段 7: Static low angle |

### 工作流建议

1. **片段 1 (18s)**: 先生成 10s → 截取最后一帧 → 用 "Image to Video" 以该帧为起始再生 10s → 后期拼接
2. **片段 3 (POV)**: Kling 可能无法完美模拟 GoPro 低角度。备选方案: 用真实宠物 POV 素材，或在 Runway Gen-3 中尝试
3. **片段 4 (iPad)**: iPad 屏幕内容建议后期合成。生成时让 Kling 专注于人物动作和情绪
4. **片段 7 (冲向镜头)**: 多生成几版。Kling 对"动物跑向镜头"的理解较好，但最后的跳跃可能需要多次尝试
5. **色调统一**: 所有片段在 DaVinci Resolve 中统一做一遍调色（暖琥珀色调 + 电影颗粒感），保证视觉连贯性
